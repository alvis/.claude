      Read this before the diff

      • Dedup keys on origin, not severity. A check that flaps warning↔critical stays one incident, and the incident's shown severity is the most recent alert's — it can appear to downgrade.
      • Cooldown withholds pages, not updates. Inside the five-minute window a re-fire still updates the incident but does not page — unless the severity is strictly higher than the last paged severity.
      • Dedup only applies to open incidents. The unique index is partial (WHERE state = 'open'), so a re-fire after resolution opens a fresh incident on purpose.
      • Two files carry the risk. dedupe.go and notifier.go decide what collapses and what stays silent; the rest is plumbing.
