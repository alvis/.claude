Where this came from

The change answered a specific on-call complaint.

The request that produced this board

“Our disk-pressure check pages the whole rotation every 30 seconds when a node is flapping. Add alert dedup and a cooldown so one flapping check is one incident and one page — but don't swallow a real escalation. I want to actually understand the change before I merge it, not just skim the diff.”

What the coder produced from it

A fingerprint-based collapse in dedupe.go, a cooldown gate with an escalation bypass in notifier.go, a partial unique index that scopes dedup to open incidents, a flap counter, and a config block. This board walks that change in risk order and then checks whether the three non-obvious behaviors actually landed.
