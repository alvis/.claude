      How to read this board

      • Placement is the answer. A card's lane, and its order within that lane, is what the reply carries — there is no separate form to fill in.
      • Two ways to move a card. Drag it with a pointer, or focus its lane selector and choose a lane; both stay in sync and both count the same.
      • Nothing is a ticket edit. This board never writes to the tracker. It produces one plan you paste back — reorder freely without consequence.
