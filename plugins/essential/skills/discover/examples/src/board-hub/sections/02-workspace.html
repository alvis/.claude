Ephemeral, but navigable

Durable enough to cross-link, temporary by design.

All boards for one discovery run live in a single session workspace directory. Because they share that directory, they can cross-link with plain relative paths and you can bookmark a board for the duration of the review.

Nothing here is permanent. Once your answers and annotations are captured in a board's single generated prompt and transferred to the evidence ledger, the whole workspace is discarded — the ledger, not these pages, remains the source of truth.
