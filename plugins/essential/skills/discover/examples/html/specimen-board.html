      Self-critique

      What this specimen buys, and where it breaks.

      Trade-offs, honestly

      Wins

      • A single accent-filled control makes the primary path obvious on first glance.
      • The brand palette is scoped to the specimen, so the tool's chrome and teaching pins stay neutral and readable.
      • Status reads as label plus colour, so the four states survive a greyscale or colour-blind check.

      Costs

      • A theme-fixed specimen will not follow the viewer's dark mode; it reads like a captured screenshot.
      • Reserving accent-soft for the revenue tile spends the brand's strongest fill on one metric.

      Fails when

      • A fifth order status appears and the fixed four-colour scale runs out of distinct, accessible hues.
      • The table has to show more than a handful of columns on a phone, where the flat layout has no room to breathe.

      Data notes

      The order rows, the 1,284 open orders, the $48,190 revenue figure, the fulfilment-flow ribbon proportions, and the stat-tile sparkline trends are invented illustrative data. Treat them as placeholder shapes, not real Meridian numbers, when building the screen.
