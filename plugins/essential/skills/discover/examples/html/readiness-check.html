      Architecture-changing unknown

      Who decides whether a notification is allowed?

      Application-owned

      Callers resolve product preference and send an already-authorized intent. The service API stays narrow; policy remains duplicated.

      Service-owned

      The service receives customer and event context, owns preference storage, and becomes the audited policy authority.

      Split ownership

      Products choose channels while the service enforces legal suppression and workspace-wide exclusions.
